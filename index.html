Our Story


Melik means "King" in the Armenian language, and sections of Armenia long ago were divided up into Melikdoms, or Kingdoms. The Melik referenced in our name is our proprietor's late Father in Law, Phillip Melik Aghjayan, who passed away from terminal illness in 2018.

The Armenian people have been through unimaginable atrocities over the years that still continue to play out on the world stage to this day. This history of standing strong in the face of unfathomable oppression and genocide has made it's mark on the culture world-wide and on the people who call that culture their own.
